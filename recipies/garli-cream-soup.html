Garlic Cream Soup

In Germany and Austria it is called knoblauchsuppe for a reason

Let's see how to make this easy recipie!

Ingridients:

  • 1 and a half liters of Chicken Stock
  • 2 cloves of Garlic
  • 3 tbs of Sunflower Oil
  • 2 tbs of Flour
  • 200 ml of Heavy Cream
  • 1 loaf of Sourdough Bread

Steps:

  1. Mince the garlic
  2. In another pan boil the chicken stock
  3. In a pan heat the oil and sate the graclic for 3 minutes(Be cautious, don't burn the garlic!)
  4. Add the sateed garclic to the boiling stock and boil for another 10 minutes
  5. Mix the flour with the heavy cream and add it to the stock and let it simmer for another 10 minutes
  6. Blend it with an immersion blender
  7. Cut the to off the bread and scoop out the inside to make a bowl
  8. BON APETITE!
