Garlic Cream Soup

In Germany and Austria, it's called Knoblauchsuppe for a reason.

Let’s learn how to make this simple and delicious recipe!

Ingridients:

  • 1 and a half liters of chicken stock
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tbsp of sunflower oil
  • 2 tbsp of flour
  • 200 ml of heavy cream
  • 1 loaf of sourdough bread

Steps:

  1. Mince the garlic.
  2. In another pan, bring the chicken stock to a boil.
  3. In a separate pan, heat the oil and sauté the garlic for 3 minutes (Be careful not to burn it!).
  4. Add the sautéed garlic to the boiling stock and let it cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. Whisk the flour into the heavy cream, then stir it into the soup. Let it simmer for 10 more minutes.
  6. Blend the soup using an immersion blender until smooth.
  7. Cut the top off the bread and scoop out the inside to make a bowl.
  8. Bon appétit!
