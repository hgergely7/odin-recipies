Lasagna recipie

Lasagna is a famous italian comfort food. Loved by everybody

Now let's see how to make it!

Ingridients:

  • 400 g Minced Beef
  • 1 can of Tomato Puree
  • 1 box of Lasagna Sheets
  • 1 tbls of Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Bechamel Souce
  • Shreaded Mozzarella

Steps:

  1. Boil 3 liters of salted water and add the lasagna sheets to the boiling water for ~3 minutes
  2. Heat the olive oil in a pan
  3. Once the oil is hot add the minced beef
  4. Salt and pepper for the tase
  5. Once the meat is browned add the canned tomatoes and simmer it for 30 minutes
  6. Now layer the sheets in a tray until we're out of ingridients
    • 1 layer of lasagna sheets
    • 1 layer of "Bolognese" souce
    • 1 layer of Bechamel souce
  7. Top with shreaded mozzarella
  8. Preheat the oven to 180 celsius and bake for30 min
  9. BON APETITE!
