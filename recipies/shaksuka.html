Shaksuka

The Middle East's favourite breakfast is really easy to make, let's see how.

Ingridients:

  • 2 canned, peeled Tomatoes
  • 1 large Onion
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 3 Eggs
  • 3 tbls of Olive Oil
  • 1 tbls of Middel Eastern Spices

Steps:

  1. Saute the chopped onion and bell peppers in the heated olive oil
  2. Add the Middle Eastern Spices and mix them
  3. Add the canned tomatoes
  4. After 10 minutes of boiling add the eggs and cover with the lid for another 5 minutes
  5. BON APETIE!
