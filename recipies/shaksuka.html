Shaksuka

The Middle East’s favorite breakfast is surprisingly easy to make. Let’s see how it’s done!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of peeled tomatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 tbls of Middel Eastern Spices

Steps:

  1. Sauté the chopped onion and bell peppers in heated olive oil.
  2. Add the Middle Eastern spices and stir well.
  3. Add the canned tomatoes.
  4. After boiling for 10 minutes, crack the eggs into the pan, cover with a lid, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Bon appétit!
